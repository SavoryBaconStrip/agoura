About Us

Your Trusted Local
Accounting Partner

Agoura Accounting is a full-service CPA firm proudly based in Agoura Hills, California. For over two decades we have provided individuals, families, and businesses with the expert financial guidance they need to thrive.

We believe accounting should be approachable. Whether you're a first-time filer, a growing small business, or a seasoned entrepreneur, our team takes the time to understand your unique situation and delivers solutions tailored to your goals—not a one-size-fits-all approach.

Our commitment to accuracy, integrity, and personalized service has made us a trusted name throughout the Conejo Valley and greater Los Angeles area.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Accounting Services

From annual tax filing to ongoing business advisory, we offer the full range of services you need to stay financially strong.

📄

Tax Preparation & Planning

Expert preparation for individuals and businesses, with proactive strategies to minimize your tax liability year-round.

  • Individual & joint returns (Form 1040)
  • Business returns (S-Corp, Partnership, LLC)
  • Multi-state tax filing
  • Year-round tax planning
📊

Bookkeeping & Accounting

Accurate, timely financial records so you always know where your business stands and are ready at year-end.

  • Monthly bookkeeping & reconciliation
  • Financial statements preparation
  • QuickBooks setup & support
  • Accounts payable & receivable
💼

Payroll Services

Reliable payroll processing that keeps your employees happy and your business compliant with all tax obligations.

  • Weekly, bi-weekly & monthly payroll
  • Payroll tax deposits & filings
  • W-2 & 1099 preparation
  • Direct deposit setup
📈

Business Advisory

Strategic financial guidance to help you make confident decisions, improve profitability, and plan for the future.

  • Business entity formation & structure
  • Cash flow analysis & forecasting
  • Budgeting & financial planning
  • Business purchase/sale consulting
🛡️

IRS Representation

Don't face the IRS alone. We represent you in audits, disputes, and collections so you can stay focused on your life.

  • Audit representation
  • IRS & state tax notices
  • Offer in compromise
  • Penalty abatement requests
🏡

Estate & Trust Tax

Careful, compassionate guidance on estate and trust tax matters to preserve your legacy and protect your family.

  • Estate tax returns (Form 706)
  • Trust tax returns (Form 1041)
  • Inheritance tax planning
  • Gift tax returns (Form 709)
